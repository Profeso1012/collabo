What is a Chromebook good for?

A Chromebook is a laptop you can use for just about everything, from productivity and creativity to gaming and entertainment. Whether youre a student or a working professional, Chromebooks are good for all your day-to-day tasks, including:

  • Note taking
  • Word processing
  • Spreadsheet editing
  • Presentation making
  • Video streaming
  • Gaming
  • Collaborating
  • Web browsing
  • Emailing
  • Social networking

Chromebooks are versatile, making them ideal for work, school, and play.

What is a Chromebook?

There is no difference between a Chromebook and a laptop - a Chromebook is a laptop! Unlike other laptops, Chromebooks are powered by ChromeOS, the fast and easy-to-use operating system developed by Google.

What apps can you use on a Chromebook?

Chromebooks come with the appyou need for work and play.

Apps for productivity: Get productive on your Chromebook with Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Meet, Docs, Sheets, and Slides. You can also access Microsoft 365 app on a Chromebook to use Word, Excel, PowerPoint, OneNote, OneDrive, and Outlook.

Apps for creativity: Unleash your creativity with photo editing apps like Adobe Photoshop4 and LumaFusion , video editing apps such as Clipchamp, and music creation apps like the award-winning Cubasis 3. Graphic design, illustration, and digital painting tools like Figma, Sumo Creative Suite, and Clip Studio Paint Pro are also available.

Apps for entertainment: Enjoy video streaming apps like Disney+, Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, and Twitch. You can also stream music on your Chromebook with Spotify, YouTube Music, iHeartRadio, or SoundCloud1.

Can you play games on a Chromebook?

Yes, you can play games on a Chromebook! Cloud gaming platforms like NVIDIA GeForce NOW, Xbox Cloud Gaming, Amazon Luna, Boosteroid allow you to access games on your Chromebook2. Instantly play the latest high quality games like Monster Hunter Wilds, Call of Duty: Black Ops 6, and more.

You can also play Android games on Chromebook by downloading them fromthe Play Store,including hits and classics such as3:

  • Roblox
  • Minecraft
  • Stardew Valley
